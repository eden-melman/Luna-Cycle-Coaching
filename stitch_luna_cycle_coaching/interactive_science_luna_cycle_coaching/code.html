Strength gains in the follicular phase

Higher oestrogen levels during the follicular phase may support increased strength adaptation and neuromuscular efficiency. Research indicates a significant window for hypertrophy and peak power output before ovulation.

Citation Sung et al., 2014, SpringerPlus

Perceived effort in the luteal phase

Sessions can feel harder due to elevated core temperature and progesterone influence on metabolic rate.
