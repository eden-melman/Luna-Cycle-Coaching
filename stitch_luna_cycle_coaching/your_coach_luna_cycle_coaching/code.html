THE GENESIS

"Luna Cycle Coaching started in a London gym..."

It wasn't built on aesthetics or trends. It was built on the frustration of watching high-performance athletes being coached as if they were just smaller men.

I remember the sterile fluorescent lights and the heavy iron of that basement facility where the data didn't match the reality of our biology. I realized that to unlock elite performance in women, we needed to stop fighting our cycles and start harnessing them as the ultimate ergogenic aid.

"I will never call anything a girl workout."

This is sports science. This is endocrinology. This is physiology applied with precision. We respect the complexity of the female body through rigorous discipline, not through diminishing its power.

Scientific Rigor

Coaching is not an opinion; it is a clinical practice. My methodology is anchored in validated academic research and field-tested strength protocols.
